Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Drift Boss ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to ensure the best gaming experience. This may include:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Game progress and high scores (stored locally on your device)
  • IP address and general location data for analytics purposes
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Providing and improving the game experience
  • Understanding how users interact with our website
  • Analyzing game performance and making enhancements
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users. This helps us provide you with a good experience and allows us to improve our site. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, but this may prevent some parts of our site from functioning properly.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Analytics providers (like Google Analytics) to help us understand site usage
  • Game hosting partners necessary for providing the game service
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, altered, disclosed, or used in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, and third parties who have a business need to know.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@driftboss.vip
